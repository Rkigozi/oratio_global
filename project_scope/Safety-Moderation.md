# Safety & Moderation

## Purpose

Ensure safe and respectful use of the platform.

---

## Rules

- no abuse
- no hate
- no spam
- no harmful content

---

## MVP Approach

- basic filtering
- manual moderation
- optional reporting

---

## Safeguarding

- not a crisis support service
- include disclaimer

Example:
“If you need urgent help, contact local emergency services.”

---

## Privacy

- no exact location
- anonymous supported

---

## Limitations

- no advanced moderation system
- no real-time review

---

## Guiding Principle

safe → respectful → supportive
